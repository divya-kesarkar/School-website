Athletics And Fitness

Sports are a cornerstone of our holistic approach to education, playing a crucial role in fostering a healthy lifestyle and encouraging students' active participation and engagement. Through sports, we instill values of teamwork, discipline, and resilience, preparing students not only for academic success but also for life beyond the classroom.

We offer a wide range of sports facilities to cater to diverse student interests and passions. Our facilities promote physical activity, skill development, and teamwork. Whether aspiring athletes or simply staying active, our facilities provide opportunities for all students to excel. With indoor and outdoor options, we're committed to fostering physical fitness, skill development, and sportsmanship among our students. Our focus is not only to create sportsmen for the future but to also imbibe the quality of teamwork and dedication which is instilled in our students through training them by introducing the traditional sports such as kho-kho, kabaddi, etc

Our school offers a comprehensive sports facility, including a 20,000 sqft turf, a 4-lane running track, and a 65ft x 43ft swimming pool. Together, these facilities contribute to a well-rounded sports program that encourages active lifestyles and enhances the overall well-being of our students.

At RWS, education goes beyond the classroom. Our vibrant extracurricular activities enrich lives, foster passions, and shape well-rounded individuals. Students pursue interests, develop talents, and form lasting friendships in a supportive and enriching environment.

Extracurricular Endeavours

Our life skills and value education program empowers students to navigate life's challenges with confidence, resilience, and integrity. Through interactive lessons, real-world applications, and experiential learning, students develop essential knowledge, attitudes, and behaviors for success in today's world. We foster social and emotional competence, nurture character and values, and provide practical life skills, preparing students to lead fulfilling lives and make positive contributions to society.

Each semester, we organize parent conferences to offer parents detailed insights into their child’s development. These meetings are designed to be engaging, allowing the parents to actively participate in discussing their child’s academic journey. Moreover, we use these sessions to set future objectives and goals collaboratively.

ACE Academy

Founded with a vision to empower students with confidence, communication skills, and artistic expression, the academy offers a range of programs and courses designed to cater to individuals of all ages and skill levels.
