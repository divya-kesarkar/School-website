Welcome to RWS

Welcome to Rumao World School, where excellence in education meets a vibrant community. With a dynamic curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and a wide range of extracurricular activities, we support students in discovering their passions and developing their talents. Explore our website to discover the myriad opportunities we offer, from cutting-edge academic programs to vibrant sports and arts initiatives.

A Comprehensive Education Rooted in Values

At RWS, our motto "Inspire, Innovate, Empower" reflects our deep commitment to holistic education. We believe that nurturing students goes beyond academic excellence, focusing on the development of essential life skills that prepare them for the real world. Our carefully designed Life Skills sessions equip students with critical interpersonal and social abilities, enabling them to handle life’s challenges with confidence and resilience.

These sessions cover various important topics. And by setting realistic and achievable goals, students learn the importance of perseverance and self-discipline.

With weekly sessions integrated into our curriculum, we ensure that our students grow into well-rounded, emotionally intelligent individuals who are ready to face the complexities of life with a balanced perspective. At RWS, we are dedicated to empowering our students not just academically but as capable, compassionate, and confident individuals in all aspects of life.
