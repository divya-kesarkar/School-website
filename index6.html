Our Infrastructure

Artistic Impression The Rumao Earth Campus is a 5-acre educational and sports complex that envisions offering exceptional learning opportunities. Situated at the heart of Vasai and Virar (west), its strategic location provides convenient access to the people. This facility is designed to cater to a wide range of educational and recreational needs, making it a valuable asset, whether for academic pursuits, sports activities, or community engagement. The Rumao Earth Campus aims to serve as a hub for growth, development, and enrichment for individuals of all ages.

Facilities

Modern Home Rooms

Classrooms at RWS are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and educational tools to enhance the learning experience for students.

Multiple Laboratories

Our cutting-edge laboratories in Science, Computer, Math, and Literacy, where students delve into hands-on experiments, coding challenges, mathematical puzzles, and literary explorations.

Dance & Music Studio

Dance room provides a nurturing and supportive environment for students to learn, express themselves, and develop physical, emotional, and social skills. Music room serves as a vibrant hub for creativity, and cultural enrichment.

Learning Centre

Learning centers are structured areas where students engage in independent learning activities.

Swimming pool

Our swimming pool of 65ft x 43ft will allow a variety of water-based physical activities and sports.

Multi-Sports Turf

A 20,000 sq.ft. turf which will offer Multi-sports benefits for all age groups, giving them varied options and recreational spaces.
