Our Structure

The RWS curriculum emphasizes a holistic, engaging, and enjoyable learning experience. Our approach aims to inspire students to become independent, lifelong learners. Shaped by CBSE aligned with NEP 2020 guidelines, our curriculum is uniquely self-paced and personalized, focusing on global issues to foster comprehensive student development. We offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities and in-house events to complement classroom learning. Through hands-on investigations at our Learning Centre, we implement a four-corner learning approach, nurturing students to thrive as independent learners.

Assessment

At the Rumao World School, as a parent, you will witness your child develop into a curious learner who will always want to learn beyond the classroom and become a global citizen of the 21st century. We have assessments planned to record the attainment of key skills such as communication, problem solving, leadership, creative thinking, good literacy, and good numeracy skills.
